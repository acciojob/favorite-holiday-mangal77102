Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, India. It is situated on the banks of the Beas River and is surrounded by snow-covered mountains, dense forests, and lush green valleys. Manali is famous for its pleasant climate, scenic views, and adventure activities like trekking, paragliding, skiing, and river rafting. Popular tourist attractions in Manali include Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Hadimba Temple, and Old Manali. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and honeymoon couples.

  1. Solang Valley – A famous spot for adventure sports like paragliding and skiing.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for its snow-covered mountains and breathtaking views.
  3. Hadimba Devi Temple – An ancient wooden temple surrounded by tall cedar trees.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its peaceful atmosphere, cafes, and culture.
  5. Beas River – Famous for river rafting and scenic natural beauty.
  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread stuffed with local spices.
  • Dham – A complete festive meal served during special occasions in Himachal.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup influenced by Tibetan cuisine, perfect for cold weather.
  • Chana Madra – A yogurt-based curry made with chickpeas and spices.
  • Babru – A Himachali-style stuffed bread similar to kachori.
