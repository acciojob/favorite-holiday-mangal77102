Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its snow-covered mountains, lush green valleys, and pleasant climate. Manali is a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and travelers looking for peace.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports like skiing and paragliding.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for its stunning snow views.
  3. Hadimba Devi Temple – An ancient wooden temple surrounded by forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its calm atmosphere and cafes.
  5. Beas River – Ideal for river rafting and scenic beauty.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Dham – A local festive meal served on special occasions.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup perfect for cold weather.
  • Chana Madra – A yogurt-based curry with rich flavors.
  • Babru – A stuffed Himachali bread similar to kachori.
