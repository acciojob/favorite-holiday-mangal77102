Manali


Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its snowy mountains, green valleys, and pleasant weather, making it a perfect holiday destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure activities.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for snow-covered views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – An ancient wooden temple.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its peaceful vibe.
  5. Beas River – Ideal for river rafting.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional steamed bread.
  • Dham – A local festive meal.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup.
  • Chana Madra – A yogurt-based curry.
  • Babru – A stuffed Himachali bread.
