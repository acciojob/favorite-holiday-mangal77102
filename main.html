Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is famous for its snow-covered mountains and pleasant climate.

  1. Solang Valley – Known for adventure sports.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Famous for snowy landscapes.
  3. Hadimba Temple – An ancient wooden temple.
  4. Old Manali – Loved for its peaceful vibe.
  5. Beas River – Ideal for river rafting.
  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali bread.
  • Dham – A festive local meal.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup.
  • Chana Madra – A yogurt-based curry.
  • Babru – A stuffed local bread.
